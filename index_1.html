I help community legal centres, advocacy organisations, for-purpose organisations and industry associations across Australia identify and address systemic issues through well-evidenced, politically credible law reform campaigns. I have over 20 years' multi-sector experience across government, private legal practice, the United Nations and international NGOs. Having led the Australian Law Reform Commission, the Queensland Law Reform Commission and worked with parliamentary committees, I know exactly the drivers that will inspire a reform body to effective action. I have developed law reform recommendations in some of the most complex areas of law and legal policy, including elder abuse, financial services law, class actions procedures and corporate crime.
